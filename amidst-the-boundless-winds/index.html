Amidst the Boundless Winds


Amidst the boundless winds, my quest unfurled,
Through valleys deep, where whispers swirled,
In the moon’s caress, a road was spread in sight,
As stars held their secrets in the tranquil night.

Beneath the sky’s expanse, a canvas vast,
The answers to life’s questions there I cast.
In every zephyr and rustling of the trees,
I heard the ancient tales upon the breeze.

With open heart and eyes to the unknown,
I found a world where truths are softly shown.
In the boundless winds, the answer’s grace,
Reveals itself in nature’s sweet embrace.

Darkness melted away from the dawning sky,
Is it so? Pausing the steps, I let out a gentle sigh.
For as the earth’s melody evermore confides:
Amidst the boundless winds, the answer resides.

在那茫茫的風裡


我展開追尋啊，在那茫茫的風裡；
呢喃的低語盤桓旋繞，漫漫於幽幽深谷。
道路在月光柔和的輕拂下，於眼前婆娑蜿蜒；
沉寧靜謐的夜幕中，璀璨星辰緘默無語。

無垠的蒼穹下，是張廣袤的畫布；
人生的深意啊，我仰問蒼天。
和風徐徐、枝葉沙沙；
那千年來的故事，我聆聽著微風娓娓訴說。

當我敞開心胸、直直凝視著未知；
才發現真諦輕輕地，浮現於世。
答案的高雅啊，在那茫茫的風裡；
顯現於自然的溫情懷抱。

黑暗在破曉的天空中消融漸去；
原來是如此嗎？我停下腳步，輕輕嘆了口氣。
因為正如同大地的旋律永遠所傾吐的：
答案啊，在那茫茫的風裡。

It’s a introspective and evocative poem that reflects a personal journey of self-discovery and contemplation. It serves as a lyrical exploration of the interconnectedness between the natural world and the human experience. The inspiration was drawn from moments of solitude and reflection in the midst of nature.

Amidst the boundless winds, the answer resides.

The juxtaposition of the boundless winds, with their vast and limitless expanse, against the notion of discovering an elusive “answer,” serves as a captivating contrast, illuminating the profound concept that amidst the boundless and seemingly turbulent, there lies the potential for concealed and meaningful truths to emerge.
